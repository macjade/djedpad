About Us

Djedpad's Mission

Djedpad is a Cardano project incubator offering token IDOs which provide funding for new projects within the cardano ecosystem.

  • Connect

    Djedpad will connect early stage Cardano innovators and projects with crypto enthusiasts who wish to get in on a project early.

  • Cardano Focused

    Djedpad is the first Cardano Focused Incubator and Launchpad projects being built on the Cardano network.

  • After Launch Support

    Developers building on the Cardano Network can utilize Djedpad to ideate, market and launch their ideas while having early support from a community of backers.

How It Works

How To Participate

Djedpad will work in a simple and easy to understand process.

Connect Wallet

Our Platform will work on a simple wallet connect for users, as a Decentralized Platform. Users would not require KYC to get involved with us. All they need is a supported Cardano Wallet (Nami, Gero Wallet etc) to get started.

Stake $DJP

$DJP token holders who are staking their tokens will qualify for the Guaranteed allocations to early sales of projects launching through Djedpad Launchpad.

Participate in IDO’s

Once you users must have Connected their wallets, Staked their $DJP tokens and must have gotten their guaranteed allocation, all users need to do is choose the Projects they wish to participate in and follow their participation details.

Apply for IDO

Get access to huge set of tools to seamlessly handle your project's integration with blockchain.
